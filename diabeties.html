Diabetes Risk Assessment

1. Family History: Do you have a family history of diabetes?

2. Age: What is your age?

3. Weight and BMI: What is your Body Mass Index (BMI)?

4. Physical Activity: How often do you engage in moderate to vigorous physical activity?

5. Diet: How often do you consume sugary drinks or high-sugar foods?

6. Symptoms: Have you experienced any of the following symptoms recently? (Select all that apply)

7. Blood Pressure: What is your average blood pressure reading?

8. Cholesterol Levels: Have you been diagnosed with high cholesterol or triglycerides?

9. Gestational Diabetes: If applicable, have you ever been diagnosed with gestational diabetes during pregnancy?

10. Sleep Habits: How many hours of sleep do you get on average per night?

11. Smoking and Alcohol: Do you smoke or consume alcohol regularly?

12. Stress Levels: How would you describe your stress levels?

13. Previous Diagnosis: Have you ever been told by a doctor that you have prediabetes or borderline diabetes?

14. Waist Circumference: What is your waist circumference?

15. Ethnicity: What is your ethnicity?
